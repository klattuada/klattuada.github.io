CSC 106 Final Project

This is my final program for CSC 106. This program is the game plinko that was created with my partner in Javascript. To play: first Clicks the Select Color button that will bring them to the color select screen. Choose the color of the ball and then click the Play button. Then click anywhere at the top of the screen to drop a ball. Dropping the ball will cost $5 each time. The goal is to get as much money as possible!
